Character Biography

Mario

Real Name: Mario Mario


Destined for greatness as just a small child, Mario grew up alongside his brother Luigi and five other siblings. They lived in the New Donk City later moving to Mushroom Kingdom. Mario has two jobs, firstly he’s a Plumber and secondly, he is to keep Princess Peach safe from her kidnappers.

Strengths and Abilities

  • Superhuman Strength
  • Superhuman Speed
  • Superhuman Reflexes
  • Superhuman Stamina
  • Superhuman Agility
  • Martial Arts
  • Firebrand
  • Super Jump Punch
  • Mario Tornado
  • Cape
  • Toon Force
  • 4th Wall Awareness
  • Fireball

Luigi

Real Name: Luigi Mario


Brother of Mario, Luigi grew up in the same place as his Plumber Brother. Luigi is the deuteragonist of the Super Mario Series. He is Heroic and a very kind and sensitive character. Unlike his brother, Luigi is shy and sometimes goofy and at times can be naïve.

Strengths and Abilities

  • Power Jump
  • Green Fireball
  • Green Missile
  • Super Jump Punch
  • Luigi Cyclone
  • Thunder Hand

Princess Peach

Real Name: Princess Peach Toadstool


Princess Peach Toadstool is commonly the damsel in distress, awaiting her hero Mario to save her from her kidnappers. Peach is a sweet and well-mannered Princess who loves dancing and gardening among many other things.

Strengths and Abilities

  • Heart Power
  • Hovering
  • Levitation
  • Therapy
  • Group Hug
  • Sleepy Time
  • Come Back
  • Mute
  • Psych Bomb
  • Weakening

Toad

Real Name:Toad


Toad is another one of Princess Peach's long-time protectors of the Mushroom Kingdom, although a bit of a coward and usually fails in saving her. He is a single character among an entire species of look-alikes. As a supporting character he tries to help Mario and Luigi on their adventures. Toad is very tiny, with little arms and tiny toes and he’s always smiling

Strengths and Abilities

  • Superhuman Strength
  • Superhuman Speed
  • Vehicular Drive
  • Plant Manipulation
  • Pyrokinesis
  • Cryokinesis

Bowser

Real Name: King Bowser Koopa


Bowser is the antagonist of most of the Super Mario Series’, always trying to kidnap the perfect Princess Peach, much to Mario and his fellow friends dismay. He is a big scary Turtle like character who breaths fire from his mouth.

Strengths and Abilities

  • Fire Breathing
  • Superhuman Strength
  • Superhuman Durability
  • Superhuman Speed and Agility
  • Superhuman Stamina
  • Dark Magic
  • Size Manipulation
